About Me

My name is Frida Andersson and I’m a MSE student at Interaction Technology and Design at Umeå University. I’m becoming a UX/UI-designer and web development and interaction designer when taking my degree year 2021.

I would describe myself as a happy, outgoing and social person, always with a smile on my face and close to laughter. I like challenges in life, to learn new things and experience new contexts.
In my spare time, I am interested in keeping my body in trim with training of various kinds, downhill skiing and orienteering is just few of them. To travel, meet new people and new cultures is something that I value. I’m also a creative person who’s not afraid to try new things, design and develop projects from start to finish.

During my time at the scouts I have learned to be a person how have the ability to work efficiently in a group as well as an individual. I would say that have the quality to engage and make people driven to achieve the goals.
